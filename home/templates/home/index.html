Our Shop

Our History UNIQUE Crafts & Design was established by 2 friends, Kate and Magdalena, who shared the same passion for original designed handcrafts.
What has been a dream for them for years, in July 2010 they set up a shop in Dublin city centre, in George’s Street Arcade, Ireland’s first purpose shopping centre and one of Europe’s oldest city markets.
Their love and enthusiasm for beautiful handmade products can be seen in the wide range of products offered in the Dublin shop and online.
Unique is a small business that prides itself on delivering beautiful, colourful handcrafted items at affordable prices promoting at the same time many creative multi talented individuals living in Ireland

Our Products

Our shop is located in the George’s Street Arcade, Dublin’s first purpose built Victorian Shopping Centre (1881), and one of Europe’s oldest, located in the heart of Dublin city centre in the cultural quarter area.
